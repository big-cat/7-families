7 Families is a charity led campaign to provide a tax-free
income for one year to seven people who have lost their
income because of a serious or long-term illness or disability.

ITV’s Divya Kohli meets with Tracey Clarke and husband Tim, to see how their lives have been affected through Tracey’s loss of sight and how 7 Families has helped them throughout 2015.
